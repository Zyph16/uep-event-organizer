About Us

UEP Facility Booking System is a web-based platform that simplifies how students and staff reserve university facilities. It's built to replace manual, paper-based booking with a faster, more organized process. This system was developed to eliminate manual processes, reduce paperwork, and minimize scheduling conflicts that often occur with traditional booking methods. By going digital, we ensure faster approvals, real-time availability, and better coordination across departments.

What It Offers
  • Online booking for rooms and venues
  • Real-time availability checking
  • Auto-notification and updates
  • Conflict-free Scheduling
